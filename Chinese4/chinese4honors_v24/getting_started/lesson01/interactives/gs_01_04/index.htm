Menu

Using the menu on the left allows you to navigate between modules.

Toolbox

Use the Toolbox to access a searchable page where you can find the supporting documents for any lessons in this course.

Glossary

Stop! Don’t google it, and don’t translate it! You can find it here. The glossary has most of the words in the lessons. You can search from English to Chinese or Chinese to English.

Print

Using the Print button allows you to print the lesson page.

Objectives

Why? Why do I have to learn this? The objectives are the "why" behind the "what." You can find all the objectives on this tab to help you direct your learning and studying. They even update with each page, so you can be sure exactly what the goal is for each lesson.

Speech Tools

To master a language, you should speak it. You will have many opportunities to practice your Chinese. If you are unable to access the speech tool, you can find other options here to complete the recordings. Don’t hesitate to reach out to your teacher if you have a concern.

Module Progress Bar

Using the module progress bar will help you see how much of the lesson you have completed already. You can also navigate to other pages within the lesson from the module progress bar.

Home and Arrows

Using the Home icon will be the fastest route back to the course home page. The left arrow navigates to the previous page, and the right arrow navigates to the next page.

Note Guide

Each lesson begins with a note guide for you to download to assist you as you work through the lesson. It will help you prepare for your assessments.
